This is the emblem of [[lore:the Thing]]. When its heart is destroyed, its essence becomes stored in this locket. While wearing this locket, you can cast [[Magic Jar]] from it, using the locket as the container. While your soul inhabits the locket, you can cast the following spells from it without a spell slot or components: [[Control Thoughts]], [[Detect Thoughts]], [[Dream]], and [[Telekinesis]].

If you cast [[Magic Jar]] from the locket, it isn't destroyed when the spell ends. If the locket is destroyed, the Hollow Heart appears somewhere within the caverns and the Thing is resurrected.

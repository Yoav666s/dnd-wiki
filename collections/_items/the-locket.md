This is the emblem of [[lore:the Thing]]. When its heart is destroyed, its essence becomes stored in this locket. While wearing this locket, you can't be targeted by Divination Spells or perceived by Scrying sensors unless you allow it. You can cast [[Magic Jar]] from the Locket, using it as the container. While your soul inhabits the locket, you can cast the following spells from it without a spell slot or components: [[Control Thoughts]], [[Detect Thoughts]], [[Dream]], and [[Telekinesis]].

If you cast [[Magic Jar]] from the locket, you can choose not to destroy it when the spell ends. If the locket is destroyed, the Hollow Heart appears somewhere within the caverns and the Thing is resurrected.

Den'sall has a narrow border with Briss to the west, which is fortunate because its inhabitants find the Brissian culture heretical. It is the least developed country of Cortenmaas, and by no mistake. The Den'sallian people are mostly nomads who drift from oasis to oasis, worshipping the desert itself.

Den'sall has a narrow border with Briss to the west, which is unfortunate because its inhabitants find the Brissian culture heretical. It is the least developed country of Cortenmaas, and by no mistake. Apart from settlers, the Den'salli people are mostly nomads who drift from oasis to oasis, worshipping the desert itself. They travel in parties and sustain themselves using magic to create food and water, but never try to reshape the desert to be anything else.

There are no cities in Den'sall, only settlements of cattle and tents. They lead simple lives, but are very self-sufficient. With nothing to rely on but magic and fine craftsmanship, the people of this land are highly compotent. They have no army, no protection, so they train to defend themselves from bandits.

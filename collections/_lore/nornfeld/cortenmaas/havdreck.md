Havdreck is a mountainous nation located within the desert continent of Cortenmaas. Its terrain is defined by a series of elevated rock formations and mountain ranges that rise directly from the surrounding sands. These formations vary in height and density, creating a landscape of ridges, plateaus, narrow passes, and steep inclines.

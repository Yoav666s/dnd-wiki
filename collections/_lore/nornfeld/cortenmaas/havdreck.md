Havdreck is a mountainous nation located within the desert continent of Cortenmaas. Its terrain is defined by a series of elevated rock formations and mountain ranges that rise directly from the surrounding sands. These formations vary in height and density, creating a landscape of ridges, plateaus, narrow passes, and steep inclines.

Surface water is limited and typically found in enclosed or shaded environments. Small basins, rock depressions, and subterranean reservoirs collect and retain water where evaporation is reduced. These areas support localized plant life, including drought-resistant shrubs, deep-rooted trees, and short-lived seasonal growth following infrequent rainfall. Outside of these zones, vegetation is minimal.

Havdreck contains a significant population of brass dragons, which inhabit mountainous regions, particularly areas that are difficult to access by conventional means. These dragons maintain defined territories, typically centered around lairs located in high-altitude or enclosed rock formations. Their presence influences settlement patterns, travel routes, and regional stability. Some areas remain uninhabited due to proximity to dragon territory, while others coexist within its boundaries under varying conditions of tolerance or avoidance.

Some rangers seek to master weapons to better protect civilization from the terrors of the wilderness. Members of the Hunter Conclave learn specialized fighting techniques for use against the most dire threats, from rampaging ogres and hordes of orcs to towering giants and terrifying dragons.

# Level 3: Arcane Student
You gain the following benefits.

**Arcane Scholar.** You gain a bonus to Intelligence checks equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of +1)

**Cantrips.** You learn two cantrips of your choice from the Wizard spell list. They count as Ranger spells for you. 

# Level 3: Arcane Warden Spells
When you reach a Ranger level specified in the Arcane Warden spells table, you thereafter always have the listed spells prepared.

# Level 3: Spectral Strike
You can imbue your strikes with arcane energy. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an attack, you deal an additional 1d6 Force damage.

The damage die increases to 1d8 when you reach level 7, to 1d10 when you reach level 11, and to 1d12 when you reach level 15.

# Level 7: Strong Concentration 
You gain proficiency in Constitution saving throws. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency in Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throws (your choice). 

# Level 11: Seamless Casting 
When you take the attack action, you can forgo one of your attacks to cast a cantrip that has a casting time of 1 action.

# Level 15: Arcane Protector
You can cast [[Shield]] without expending a spell slot. You can do so a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

You can use your reaction to cast [[Shield]] on another creature you can see within 30 feet when it gets hit with an attack.

The Marksman is a master gunslinger who wields firearms like it's an art. Their aim is unmatched, and their reflexes are quick.

# Level 3: Deadeye Precision
You gain the following benefits.

**Firearm Specialist.** You ignore the Loading property of firearms.

**Snap Correction.** When you make a ranged attack with a firearm, you can add your Wisdom modifier to the attack roll. You can do so after you roll the d20, but before the DM says whether the attack hits or misses. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a short or long rest.

# Level 3: Sharp Vision
You gain the following benefits.

**Bonus Proficiency.** You gain proficiency in the Perception skill. If you already proficient in it, you gain expertise.

**Quickdraw.** You gain a bonus to initiative rolls equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of +1).

# Level 7: Eye for Detail
Whenever you make a Wisdom (Perception) check, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.

# Level 10: Aim Correction
When you use your Snap Correction feature, you can reroll the d20 once and use the highest roll.

# Muscle Memory 
Starting at 15th level, when you roll for initiative and don't have any remaining uses of Snap Correction, you regain one use.

# Deadly Aim
Starting at 18th level, you deal additional damage with firearms equal to your Wisdom modifier.

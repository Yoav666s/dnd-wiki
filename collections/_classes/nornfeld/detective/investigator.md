# Forced Opening
Starting at 3rd level when you choose this field, when you take the [[Help]] action to aid an ally in attacking a creature, the target of that attack takes an additional 2d6 damage of the attack's type.

This damage dice change when you reach certain levels in this class. They become 2d8 when you reach 7th level, 2d10 when you reach 13th level, and 2d12 when you reach 17th level.

# Thoughtful Phrasing
Also starting at 3rd level, you gain a bonus to Charisma checks equal to your Intelligence modifier.

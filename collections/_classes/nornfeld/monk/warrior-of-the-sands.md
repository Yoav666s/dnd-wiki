Warriors of the Sands are shaped by exposure to unforgiving landscapes where heat, wind, and scarcity erode weakness and reward precision. Their discipline reflects the shifting nature of the desert itself--fluid in motion, sudden in violence, and relentless in endurance. These monks learn to read the terrain as an extension of their own bodies, gliding across loose ground without hesitation and turning every grain beneath their feet into an advantage.

# Level 3: One with the Wastes
Your martial arts techniques adapt to the harsh reality of the desert. You gain the following abilities.

**Dune Walker.** You ignore difficult terrain composed of sand or loose earth.

**Savage Swipe.** Whenever you deal slashing damage with a Scimitar to a creature, you can roll the damage die twice and use either roll against the target.

**Slashing Flurry.** You can use the mastery property of Scimitars. Whenever you make an attack granted by your Flurry of Blows, you can use a Scimitar in place of an unarmed strike.

# Level 6: Dirty Fighting
Once per turn, immediately before you take the Attack action while standing on ground composed of sand or loose earth, you can expend 1 Focus point to force a creature you can see within 5 feet of you to make a Dexterity saving throw. On failure, the creature has the Blinded condition until the end of its next turn. You can also use this feature anywhere by expending a handful of sand from a pouch you carry, but you must have a free hand to do so.

# Level 11: Crescent Riposte
When you use your Deflect Attacks feature, if you reduce the damage of a melee attack to 0 and expend 1 Focus Point to redirect the attack, immediately afterwards you can make an Unarmed Strike or melee weapon attack with a Scimitar against the attacker.

# Level 17: Deep Cuts
Whenever you deal slashing damage to a creature, you deal additional damage equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of 1).

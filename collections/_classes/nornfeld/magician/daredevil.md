# Showman's Bravado
Starting at 3rd level, you gain a bonus to Dexterity (Acrobatics) and Strength (Athletics) checks equal to your Charisma modifier.

# Stage Fighting 
Starting at 3rd level, your unarmed strikes have the finesse property and their base damage equals to your Trickery die.

# Close-up Action
Starting at 6th level, you learn the following magic trick.

**Strike With Momentum.** When you take the dash action, you can expend a Trickery die and use your bonus action to make a melee attack. Roll the die and add the number rolled to the attack roll.

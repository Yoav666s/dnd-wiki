As an urban gnome, you have a natural knack for traversing the industrial streets of Morgenfell.

# Urban Gnome Traits
As an urban gnome, you have the following traits.

## Ability Score Increase
Your Dexterity score increases by 1.

## Danger Aversion
You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks when taking the hide action in combat.

## Seamless Nimbleness
You have proficiency in the Sleight of Hand or Stealth skill.

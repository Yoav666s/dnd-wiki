*Origin Feat*

Your innate agility sets you apart. You gain the following benefits.

**Speed Increase.** Your walking speed is increased by 5 feet. 

**Acrobatic Agility.** You have advantage on Dexterity ([[Acrobatics]]) checks made to maintain your movement when navigating difficult terrain, balancing, or avoiding obstacles.

*Origin Feat*

You have learned methods to provide first aid to humanoids in a critical state.  You gain the following benefits.

- When making a Wisdom (Medicine) check to stabilize a humanoid with 0 hit points, treat a roll of 4 or lower on the d20 as a 5 (unless it's a 1).
- When you successfully stabilize a creature with 0 hit points, it gains 1 hit point.

*Origin Feat (Perquisite: Spellcasting or Pact Magic Feature)*

Your combat training gives you the following benefits.

**Ceremonial Weapon.** You can perform a 1-minute ceremony with a Simple or Martial weapon, turning it into a conduit for your magic. You can use this weapon as a Spellcasting Focus for your spells. If you perform this ceremony again with a different weapon, the previous weapon loses this property.

**Martial Training.** You gain proficiency with martial weapons and light armor.

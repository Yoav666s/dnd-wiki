*Prerequisite: The ability to cast at least one spell*

Your combat training gives you the following benefits.

- You can perform a 1-minute ceremony with a Simple or Martial weapon, turning it into a conduit for your magic. You can use this weapon as a Spellcasting Focus for your spells. If you perform this ceremony again with a different weapon, the previous weapon loses this property.
- You gain proficiency with martial weapons and training with light armor.

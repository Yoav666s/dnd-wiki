*General Feat (Perquisite: Level 4+, Intelligence 13+)*

You're well practiced in tinkering. You gain the following benefits:

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence score by 1 to a maximum of 20.

**Expert Tinkerer.** You gain expertise with Tinker's Tools.

**Efficient Tinkering.** You can use Tinker's Tools to craft and repair devices (such as firearms and ammunition) at half the cost.

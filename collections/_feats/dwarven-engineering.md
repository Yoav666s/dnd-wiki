*General Feat (Perquisite: Dwarf)*

You're well studied in the development of dwarven technology. You gain the following benefits:

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence score by 1 to a maximum of 20.

**Expert Tinkerer.** You gain expertise with Tinker's Tools.

**Efficient Tinkering.** You can use Tinker's Tools to craft and repair dwarven devices (such as firearms, ammunition, or even vehicles) at half the cost.
